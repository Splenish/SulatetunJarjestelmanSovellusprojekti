Who are we?

We are a group of students with alot of passion to provide market leading software!
Our promise is to provide best quality GPS based tracking software for large companies who need to keep track of their physical resources.

What is our product?

Our product is a solution for companies who have alot of physical resources they need to track such as cars.
We provide the interface to track your physical resources using our own website and "Units".

An unit is a simple device that will track its location using an GPS module. It also tracks other information using sensors.
These units can be modified depending on the company's needs. Normal version includes temperature, humidity, location and speed tracking.

In addition there is possibility of small communication between the user of website and the holder of the unit.
You can send messages to the unit holder which he can see them as seen which will show up on the website.

Future plans

We have plans to include features such as route planning and calculating in the future!
